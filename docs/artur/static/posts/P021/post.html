Whosoever lifts this hammer with the strength of one hand over his head, if he be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor Bjornsson. Maybe.

I put an unnecessarily large amount of effort to create what is essentially a rather unpractical dumbbell. Cant say tho that it is not cool to work out with, which is sort of the point :D

The handle is 50mm in diameter and the whole hammer weights just about 35kg. Due to very wide grip this weight is already a challenge to maneuver. The materials used are 6mm steel plates and a pipe welded together and filled with lead.

The project is not over yet and will be continued :) the update will include a stylish hammer hanger and a lot of etched ornaments. See you in next update!
